Ithaca's

Apple Harvest Festival
October 3 - 5, 2014

Ithaca's Apple Harvest Festival is an annual three day celebration of food, fun, and apples! Join us to enjoy plenty of apple's, local produce, fresh baked goods, family entertainment, games, rides, prizes, and live performances on two stages.

Dates


  • Friday, October 3rd (12PM - 6PM)
  • Saturday, October 4th (10AM-6PM)
  • Sunday, October 5th (10AM-6PM)
