Websafe and Google Fonts

How to use websafe and Google Fonts on this website.

What are websafe fonts?

All websafe fonts are supported on this website and on DeviantART.

What are Google Fonts?

All fonts on Google Fonts are supported on this website and on DeviantART. To use a Google font, make sure that the spelling and capitalization are exactly correct. For example, to use a Google font named "Fjalla One", please enter "Fjalla One", and not "fjalla one" or "Fjalla one", etc.
